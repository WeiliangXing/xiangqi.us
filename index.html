About us

Some words about us

Xiangqi, or Chinese chess, a strategy board game for two players, has a history more than 3,500 years. Our club, located in Long Island , is one of the largest xiangqi clubs in United States. More than two thousands of regular members make us stronger, well-organized, and best.

  • - We deliver essence of Xiangqi culture
  • - We spread peace, love and healthy life
  • - We enjoy thousands years of ancient wisdom
